2. How to use it

2
Attach the template to a new chat
Drag peec_sphere_template.html into the chat.
3
Enter your project name & copy the prompt
Type your Peec project name below — the prompt updates automatically. Then hit copy and paste it into Claude.
4
Download the HTML, open in your browser
Claude hands you back a finished file. Double-click, done.
